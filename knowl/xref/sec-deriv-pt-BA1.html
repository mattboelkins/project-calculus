Learning objectives for this activity
 4 
  • understand each of the components of the limit definition of the derivative: \(h\text{,}\) \(a\text{,}\) \(a+h\text{,}\) \(f(a)\text{,}\) \(f(a+h)\text{,}\) and the limit itself
  • understand that the tangent line is a limit of secant lines
  • interpret the derivative’s value as the slope of the tangent line to the curve, as well as the function’s instantaneous rate of change at the fixed \(x\)-value
